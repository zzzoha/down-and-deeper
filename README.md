# down-and-deeper

Down and deeper is a pixel game, featuring two characters: Lumes and Lumina. It starts where the two were playing and then Lumina, exhausted, falls asleep. In her dreams (Lumina doesn't know she is dreaming) she descends into a cavern with a box, which she curiously goes to, leading to a prophecy written on a scroll to pop up.By using the hints to finish the blanks in the prophecy, leads the player to pick the correct path, progressing them to the next part and if not chosen correctly, leads the game to be over and Lumina trapped.

After choosing the correct path, Lumina once again decsends even lower into the scorching magma and lava of the earth. She then comes across a message in a bottle and with the help of her brother, Lumes- who narrates (he isn't actually physically there; he is thorugh Lumina's own imagination of her dreams), she deciphers the code and then has to choose a choice. 

Depending on which choice you choose, changes the way her story ends. Whether she gets to escape and wake up to the earth once more or remain trapped in her nightmares is now in the hands of you!
